ML Vis Seminar

The creation of learning classifiers requires an iterative process that is often time-consuming for machine learning and domain experts alike. There are many challenges involved in selecting the learning method, labeling data items, incrementally tuning a classifier, and assessing its effectiveness. This seminar aims at exploring how visualization can be used to help humans to understand and train better models.
